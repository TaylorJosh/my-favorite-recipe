Sweet Buttermilk Cornbread



Needed: Mixing Bowl (x2), Whisk , 8x10 Baking Dish

This is the only cornbread reciper that you'll ever need!

This battle tested recipe has been passed down for several generations, so consider yourself family. This recipe is versitle enough to be used for dressing, hush puppies, or the perfect side for Sunday dinner. Every Sunday after church, my grandmother would make this cornbreadas a side. As a child, I always though it was cake, so I would eat slice after slice, until it was all gone. As I began to cook for myself, this was the first recipe that I sought out to master. After a few tries, it became second nature. I hope this recipe brings your family as many joyful memories as it has brought mine.

Disclaimer: This is a sweet recipe, so if you prefer a more savory cornbread, please reduce the amount of sugar by 1/2, and do not add honey.
