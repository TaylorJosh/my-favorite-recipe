Directions to make Sweet Buttermilk Cornbread:

Please make sure you have all of the ingredients before getting started.

  1. (Combine dry ingredients) - In a big bowl combine: Cornmeal, Flour, Sugar, Baking Powder, Salt
  2. Stir until well blended
  3. (Combine liquid ingredients) - In a (seperate) big bowl combine: Buttermilk, Eggs, Butter, Oil, Honey
  4. Stir until well blended
  5. Combine the dry and wet ingredients together
  6. Stir until well blended
  7. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees
  8. Add mixture to baking dish
  9. Bake for 45 mins

Notes:

  • Spray dish with cooking spray or line with butter before adding mixture
  • Preheat dish before adding mixture
  • Mix wet and dry ingredients in seperate bowls before combining
  • For best results, use 8x10 baking dish
  • Allow oven to preheat for at least 10 mins
  • After 45 mins, insert a toothpick or paring knife into the center of the cornbread to see if the crumb has set.
    • If the tester comes out clean, it's done.
    • If it comes out gummy or with crumbs clinging to it, the cornbread needs more time in the oven
