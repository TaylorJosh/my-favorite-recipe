Ingredients for Sweet Buttermilk Cornbread

Dry Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 Cups Of Cornmeal
  • 1/2 Cup Of All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 to 1-1/4 Cup Of White Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Of Baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp of Salt

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Of Buttermilk
  • 2 Battered Eggs
  • 1 Stick Of Melted Butter
  • 1/3 Cup Of Vegetable Oil
  • 3 Tbs Of Honey
